Instructions for getting nps route credentials

- Go to [https://www.nps.gov/subjects/developer/get-started.htm](https://www.nps.gov/subjects/developer/get-started.htm) and fill out the form
- Copy and paste the following into your .env file
    - NPS_API_KEY = YOUR API KEY
    - NPS_BASE_URL = https://developer.nps.gov/api/v1
